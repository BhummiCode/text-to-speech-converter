Welcome to Our Text-to-Speech Converter

About This Tool

Our Text-to-Speech (TTS) Converter allows you to transform written text into spoken words. This tool leverages the power of modern web technologies to provide a seamless and interactive experience.

Features

  • Ease of Use: Simply input your text and hit the "Speak" button to hear it spoken aloud.

How It Works

  1. Enter Text: Type or paste the text you want to convert into the provided text area.
  2. Select Voice and Speed: Choose your preferred voice and adjust the speech speed using the available options.
  3. Button: Click the "Speak" button to hear your text spoken aloud.
  4. Listen: Your text will be converted to speech, and you can listen through your device's speakers or headphones.

Technologies Used

This TTS converter is built using HTML for structure, CSS for styling, and JavaScript for functionality. It integrates with modern web APIs to deliver high-quality speech synthesis.

Get Started

Try out the text-to-speech converter now by entering some text and clicking the "Speak" button. We hope you find this tool helpful and enjoyable to use!
